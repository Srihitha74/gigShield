Our Core Innovation

Income Mirror Engine

Instead of paying a flat ₹200 to everyone, GigShield queries what unaffected peer workers actually earned in adjacent zones during the same disruption window. Your payout = what your peers earned × your coverage ratio. Fair. Proportional. Fraud-proof.
